# **App Name**: Spice Route

## Core Features:

- Caterer Registration & Login: Secure registration and login functionality for catering service providers.
- Customer Registration & Login: Secure registration and login functionality for customers.
- Product Listing: Caterers can upload product details (name, description, price, images).
- Product Browsing & Search: Customers can browse available catering options, with search and filtering capabilities.
- Shopping Cart: Customers can add selected catering services to a cart and proceed to order placement.
- Order Placement & Management: Customers can place orders and view their order history; caterers can view and manage incoming orders.
- AI-Powered Recipe Suggestion Tool: Based on customer location and catering style preferences, suggests appropriate Indian recipes and dish combinations for special events. This helps caterers discover in-demand options and advertise their cultural specialization.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saffron (#FF9933) to reflect Indian culinary heritage; use with restraint.
- Background color: Cream (#FAF9F6), offering a warm, inviting feel and allowing the saffron primary to pop without overwhelming the eye.
- Accent color: Terracotta (#E2725B), an analogous hue for calls to action.
- Body text: 'PT Sans' for body text. Sans-serif for modern look, good legibility at various sizes.
- Headlines: 'Playfair', a high-contrast modern serif well-suited to headlines.
- Use simple, clear icons related to food and catering services; ensure icons are accessible and consistent.
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout with clear navigation to enhance user experience.
- Subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback and guide users through the ordering process.
